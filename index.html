What are the benefits of using HomeFiber products?

How do I configure my WiFi access point at home?

To configure your WiFi access point, connect it to your router using an Ethernet cable. Access the setup page via your browser and follow the on-screen instructions. HomeFiber provides easy guides and support for a smooth configuration experience.
