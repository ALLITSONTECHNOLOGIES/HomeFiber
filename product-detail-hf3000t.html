HF3000T

The AX3000 WiFi 6 EasyMesh Router with 1G Ethernet WAN and 1 GE LAN is a compact yet powerful networking solution for homes and small offices. Supporting dual-band AX-2.4G 2x2 and AX-5G 2x2, it delivers fast, stable, and efficient wireless performance ideal for streaming, browsing, and IoT connectivity. The router features 4 internal 3dB antennas for wide coverage and includes -5Kv surge-protected power supplies for enhanced durability. With EasyMesh support as both Controller or Agent, it allows flexible mesh network expansion. Powered by a robust MTK chipset, it offers reliable performance and modern connectivity features in one sleek device.

Product Description

The AX3000 WiFi 6 EasyMesh Router from Home Fiber is designed to provide blazing-fast wireless connectivity and seamless mesh coverage for modern homes and small offices. Built with the latest WiFi 6 (802.11ax) technology, this router ensures enhanced speed, capacity, and efficiency across all connected devices, making it perfect for high-bandwidth activities like 4K streaming, gaming, and smart home automation.

Equipped with dual-band AX-2.4G 2x2 and AX-5G 2x2 performance, it delivers combined wireless speeds up to 3000 Mbps. With 1 Gigabit WAN port and 1 Gigabit LAN port, it also offers reliable wired connections for devices like PCs, smart TVs, and gaming consoles. Internally housed are 4 high-efficiency 3dB antennas, ensuring strong and consistent signal coverage throughout your space.
