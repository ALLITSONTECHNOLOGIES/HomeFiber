HF5400 V2

The HF5400 V2 is a high-performance xPON ONT designed by Home Fiber to deliver ultra-fast internet and seamless wireless connectivity. It features 1 GPON WAN port and 5 x 1GE LAN ports for extensive wired networking, while the built-in AX5400 dual-band WiFi 6 ensures superior wireless coverage and speed with AX-2.4G 2x2 and AX-5G 4x4 configurations. Equipped with 6 high-gain internal antennas and a -5Kv surge-protected power supply, it guarantees network stability and protection. Powered by a Qualcomm IPQ5018 + RTL9601D chipset, it supports EasyMesh Controller functionality and complies with CE, FCC, and RoHS standards, making it ideal for modern homes and enterprises.
