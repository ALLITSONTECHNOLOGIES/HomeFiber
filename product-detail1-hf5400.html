Product Description

The AX5400 WiFi 6 EasyMesh Router from Home Fiber is a high-performance networking solution built for speed, coverage, and smart mesh integration. Designed to handle bandwidth-heavy applications, it supports seamless streaming, gaming, conferencing, and multi-device usage without compromise. With next-generation WiFi 6 technology, the router delivers exceptional dual-band performance through AX-2.4G 2x2 and AX-5G 4x4, offering speeds up to 5400 Mbps for smooth, high-speed wireless connectivity.

Equipped with a 2.5G Ethernet WAN port and 4 x 1GE LAN ports, the router ensures rapid and stable wired connections for desktops, servers, or smart TVs. Internally built with 6 high-efficiency 5dB antennas, it offers broad and consistent signal coverage. The EasyMesh feature allows the router to work as a Mesh Controller or Agent, easily expanding WiFi coverage across larger areas. Engineered with a robust Qualcomm IPQ5018 chipset and -5Kv surge-protected power supplies, it guarantees stable operation, device protection, and energy efficiency.
