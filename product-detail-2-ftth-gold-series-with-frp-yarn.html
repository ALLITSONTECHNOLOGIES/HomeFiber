2 Core FTTH Gold Series with FRP & Yarn – Durable Fiber Cable for High-Performance Home Connectivity

The 2 FTTH Gold Series with FRP & Yarn is a high-performance fiber optic cable designed specifically for Fiber to the Home (FTTH) applications. It features 2 cores of single-mode fiber, reinforced with Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) and aramid yarn for superior tensile strength and durability. This construction ensures excellent protection against mechanical stress, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor installations. The cable's compact and lightweight design allows for easy handling and quick deployment. With low insertion loss and stable optical performance, the 2 FTTH Gold Series delivers reliable, high-speed connectivity essential for modern broadband networks.
